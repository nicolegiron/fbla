Apply for a Job

PANG Airlines open it door to anyone who is looking for a job and is qualified.

Some jobs that someone may apply for are pilots, flight attendants, baggage handler, and mechanics. Each of theses job have their own criteria that someone must meet in order to get the job.


Pilot Requirements

Must have flight experience.


Must have a flying licence and certification.


A degree is not necessary but is recommended.

Flight Attendant Requirements

Must have at least a high school diploma


Must complete training and gain a certification through the Federal Aviation Administration or National Transportation Safety Board


Must get a physical and mental body check

Baggage Handler Requirements

Must be at least 18 years old


Must have at least a high school diploma


Must have a valid drivers license


Must get a physical check

Mechanic Requirements

FAA-approved training program or relevant work experience


FAA certification


Must have a degree in aeronautical engineering

If all requirements are met, enter your contact information and someone will be in touch.
