Not only do flyers get comfortable and affordable flights, but they are also rewarded with a multitude of perks. These perks include:

Free Miles

Access to airport lounge

Priority boarding / booking

Travel class upgrades

To gain access to these perks one must join the Frequent Flyer Program (FFP). With a simple payment of $12.50 a month, a member instantly gets access. Once a member, free miles can be earned through taking flights, using a co-branded credit card, and filling out surveys. After achieving 15,000 miles, members are rewarded with a free flight of 5,000 miles or less. Members can also hold these miles and save up to get higher class tickets. For example, if a member saves 30,000 miles they can get a free flight in first class of a flight 5,000 miles or less.
